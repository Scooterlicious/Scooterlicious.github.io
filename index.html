About me

The Past

Independent from my folks, I bought my first computer at the age of 13 from paper-route earnings, and immediately taught myself my first programming language.

The same magnetism that attracted me keeps me passionate about web development. The languages have changed—the syntax is different—but my love of problem-solving and my desire to express my creative vision through code has remained constant.

I'm originally from the southwest but I'm living my life-long dream of residing in New York City. I love the street food, the people watching, and the constant stream of activity...and brunch.

The Future

For many years, I worked as a Software Engineer at a large corporation and really felt "lost in the machine". I want to be somewhere my input is really valued, and I have the opportunity to learn and grow.
